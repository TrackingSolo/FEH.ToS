Welcome to ForeverHome!

ForeverHome is an app that assists users to find lakeside homes in all 50 states and bookmark them. I hope you enjoy ForeverHome and if you’d like to share properties with your friends and/ family, let me know and I’ll add them as a tester!

Getting Started

When you open ForeverHome for the first time, you will be prompted to enter your display name. This is the name your Friends & Family group members will see when you share properties with them. You will also be asked to sign in with your Google account, which enables syncing your properties to the cloud so they are never lost. This means you can use ForeverHome on different devices but don’t worry, you can always delete you account in the Admin tab.

Adding Properties:

ForeverHome works alongside the real estate apps you already use — Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, Trulia, and others.

To add a property to ForeverHome:

1. Open your real estate app and find a property you want to bookmark.

2. Tap the Share button on that listing.

3. Select ForeverHome from the share sheet. You may have to click “More” and select from ForeverHome from there.

ForeverHome will automatically extract the address, price, beds, baths, square footage, and listing URL. The app will switch to your Favorites tab and the property will appear there immediately. In the background, ForeverHome geocodes the address and identifies the nearest lake.

If you share the same property again later (for example, when the price changes), ForeverHome will update the existing record rather than create a duplicate.

Now, lets get started with the four tabs at the top of ForeverHome …

The Four Tabs:

Selection tab

The Selection tab gives you a geographic overview of all the properties you have rated. Properties are grouped by Tier, then state. You might ask “What the heck is a tier and why do I need it?” Great question! ForeverHome is designed with an eye on taxes and specifically for taxes Veteran retirees might have to pay. The Tiers are simply my ranking of the most retirement, tax-friendly states.


ForeverHome provides a list of each state (by tiers) and shows a count of how many rated properties you have there. Tap a state to expand it and see the individual properties listed.

Tapping any property from the state list opens the Property Detail overlay.

If you are a member of a Friends & Family group and have a group selected, the Selection tab will show only properties associated with that group.

Favorites tab

The Favorites tab shows all of your rated properties in a scrollable list, sorted by lakes.

Each property summary card shows:

- Property address and photo thumbnail

- Price, beds, baths, and square footage

- Your rating

- A sentiment line showing all group members' ratings (e.g., Jason: 8 | Christina: 6)

Tapping a card opens the Property Detail overlay.

Like the Selection tab, the Favorites tab gives you options to filter by.


Shared Favorites tab

The Shared Favorites tab is the collaboration hub for your Friends & Family group.

Here you can:

- Select a group from the dropdown at the top of the screen

- Sync to push your latest rated properties to the group and pull down your group members' properties

- See properties organized into two sections:

1. Not yet rated — properties your friends and family have shared with you that you haven’t yet rated (yellow background)

2. All properties rated by you and other group members

Syncing happens automatically in the background. You can also tap the Sync button to trigger an immediate sync. When a group member adds or updates a property, your SF tab will refresh automatically in real time.

To create a new group or invite someone, use the invite controls on this tab. Once a member accepts, you will both be able to see each other's rated properties.


Admin tab

The Admin tab is where you manage your account settings and financial defaults.

User Financials

Set the default values used to calculate monthly mortgage estimates across all properties:

- Interest Rate — Defaults to 7.0%. Enter your current rate or the rate you are shopping for.

- Loan Term — Defaults to 30 years.

- Down Payment — Defaults to 20%.

- Veteran Status — If you check this box, ForeverHome will calculate your property tax as $0, reflecting the tax exemption available to many Veterans.

These settings persist across sessions and apply automatically whenever you open a property.

Groups

View all groups you belong to and leave any group from this section.

Testing (developer tools)

Contains utilities for resetting data during testing, including clearing all properties and fixing data inconsistencies. These are not intended for regular use.
